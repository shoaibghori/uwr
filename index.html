Transforming THE WAY WE TEACH

United We Reach (UWR) is a non-profit organization working to improve quality of education & expand learning opportunities for children in socio-economically stressed demographics. Behind UWR is a team of patriotic Pakistanis - comprising successful business owners, tech gurus and seasoned bankers - all driven by their passion to give back to the society and make a profound impact on the unserved and underserved communities by striving for equity literacy through a sustainable, auditable, scalable, tech-based solution.

Like all responsible citizens, we take educational injustice very personally and are reforming Pakistan’s current curriculum that was designed to raise a nation of followers. Instead of building new institutions, we partner up with existing schools & address the core of the problem i.e. quality of education. With that objective in mind, we have developed a perfect teaching method by taking the best international teaching practices and combining it with our proprietary technology.
